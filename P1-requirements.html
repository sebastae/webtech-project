The purpose pf the site is to provide information about different types of flowers (that grows from the seeds sold by the distributor).

Our target group is stores who sell seeds (and perhaps flowers as well), and those just interested in flowers in general. What they need is a simple overview of the different seeds they can buy, together with more detailed info if needed. They also need a simple way of contacting the company.

The sire will be structured with an index page, a gallery of the flowers, an "about" page, a contact page, and a dynamic page with info filled in with Json.
